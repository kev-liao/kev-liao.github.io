I'm a first year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, generously supported by an NSF Graduate Fellowship. I work with Andrew Miller in the Decentralized Systems Lab.

My research focuses on developing a theory and practice of compositionality for building security-critical software. My work combines techniques from cryptography, programming languages, and verification to integrate ways of reasoning about security into the inherently compositional way that software is built.
