I'm a JD/PhD candidate at Harvard Law School and MIT (Electrical Engineering and Computer Science). At MIT, I conduct research as part of the Internet Policy Research Initiative and the Computer Science & Artificial Intelligence Laboratory. My research explores the interplay between law, technology, and society. In particular, my research focuses on legal-technical co-design, which asks how law and technology can help each other get things "right." My research has been generously supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship and a Harvey Fellowship.
